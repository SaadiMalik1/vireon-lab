# Supported Standards & Guidelines

VIREON's threat modeling and validation methodology is built upon the following industry and regulatory standards. Our design philosophy ensures that simulated anomalies and validation metrics map directly back to real-world risk management frameworks.

- **IEEE**: Standards for physiological data telemetry and medical device communications.
- **FDA**: Premarket cybersecurity guidelines for medical devices and continuous postmarket surveillance requirements.
- **NIST**: Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) for critical infrastructure and resilient embedded systems.
- **MITRE ATT&CK**: Adversarial tactics, techniques, and common knowledge mapping for standardized attack description.
- **TARA (Threat Analysis and Risk Assessment)**: Formal methodology for automotive and medical cyber-physical systems.
- **ISO 14971**: Application of strict clinical risk management to medical devices (Hazards and Harms).
- **IEC 81001-5-1**: Security activities in the product lifecycle of health software and health IT systems.
- **BLE Specifications**: Bluetooth Special Interest Group standards governing low-energy communication, MTU exchange, and pairing in medical peripherals.
