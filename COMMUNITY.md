# VIREON Community Infrastructure

Welcome to the VIREON community! This document outlines the infrastructure and resources available to contributors, researchers, and users of the VIREON neurosecurity simulation platform.

## Communication Channels

- **Discord Server**: Join our active Discord community to discuss threat models, neural dynamics, and collaborate on IDS plugin development. [Join Discord](#)
- **GitHub Discussions**: Use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/SaadiMalik1/Vireon/discussions) for long-form architectural proposals, Q&A, and showcasing your custom simulations.
- **Mailing List**: Subscribe to `vireon-announce@lists.example.com` for major release announcements and security advisories.

## Community Meetings

We host a monthly community call on the **first Thursday of every month at 16:00 UTC**.
- **Agenda & Notes**: Maintained in the [Community Meetings HackMD](#)
- **Recordings**: Published to our YouTube channel.

## Governance and Roles

VIREON operates under a meritocratic governance model:
- **Users**: Anyone utilizing the platform for research or education.
- **Contributors**: Individuals who have submitted accepted PRs (code, docs, or threat models).
- **Maintainers**: Core team members responsible for reviewing PRs, managing releases, and guiding the project's technical roadmap.

*Note: We are actively looking for domain experts in neuroscience and cryptography to join the maintainer team. See our [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for how to get involved.*

## Acknowledgment

We use the [All Contributors](https://allcontributors.org/) specification to recognize all forms of contribution, not just code. If you help with documentation, bug reports, or community support, you will be recognized in our `README.md`.
